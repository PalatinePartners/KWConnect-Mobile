Cody Gibson [Edit My Profile]

KWU Instructor
5/5 Write Review

Cody has been an agent for over 15 years and was a top-producing agent in Anchorage, Alaska where he built the Alaska Keller Williams office to nearly 200 agents. Now, based in Oregon, he is the owner and CEO of Portland Real Estate Group and United Home Group, as well as the Operating Partner of Keller Williams – Portland Central with more than 230 agents.

Cody founded Portland Real Estate Group (PRG) in fall of 2011. PRG has grown to include a Listing and Buyers’ Division, a call center, and a staff of 15. United Home Group (UHG) began in 2012 with Cody’s first expansion city in Tri Cities WA. Since then, UHG has continued to expand. Currently operating in 9 cities in 7 states. His groups have put over 400 homes into contract in 2015, are projected to put over 500 into contract in 2016, and has sold as many as 55 homes in a single month.

Cody is a highly sought-after speaker and business coach, having taught BOLD for MAPS Coaching of Keller Williams around the country. Cody is also a part of Gary Keller’s Top 100 mastermind group. Cody is a firm believer in MAPS coaching if you're looking to hire a coach.
